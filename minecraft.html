What is minecraft

Minecraft is a single and a multiplayer game. You ussually have to buy it but it is worth it! Minecraft is made out of blocks, you can also craft blocks in your inventory. There are different difficulties in minecraft like peaceful and normal and hard. Peaceful is the easist and hard is the hardest. Also there is survial and creative, in survival you have hearts and you can die and in creative you have all the blocks and you can die.

In minecraft there are mobs. Some mobs are friendly and some mobs attack you. The mobs that attack you are called monsters. To beat them you must craft tools in the crafting table. Also there are something called mods. Mods are extra stff that add new things in the game to make it fun. You can purchase mods in the market place.
